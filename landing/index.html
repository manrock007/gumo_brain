Why CtrLoop was born

We built CtrLoop because we lived this.

We run an AI-native software team. Every week our agents got sharper - and every week context still died at the handoff, work still reset to zero, and no one owned the outcome. We got tired of it. So we started building the loop we wished we had.

01

Siloed by design

Business and engineering speak different languages. Context dies at every handoff.

02

Rebuilt from zero

Every prompt, handoff and new agent re-establishes context - a cost that compounds at scale.

03

Assistant, not teammate

Today's AI reacts to prompts, lacks business context, and owns nothing it produces.

04

Linear, not a loop

Work happens in isolated tasks - so no decision ever makes the next one better.
